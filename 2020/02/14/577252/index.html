XRP flash occurs 58% collapse BitMEX said, "does not roll back or refund"

Beijing time 22:00 (UTC time 14:00), XRP / USD price BitMEX quickly fell from
$ 0.33 to $ 0.13, a drop of nearly 60%. According to BitMEX data, within a
minute, turnover surged to $ 6.0 million, followed by rapid recovery XRP price
in a matter of seconds, to close at $ 0.3277. The exchange said that during
the crash did not trigger stop-loss traders will not be rolled back or refund.

The flash after the collapse, many traders claim that they did not stop
trigger. One trader complained that they lost money in all transactions after
the flash crash.

As the third largest currency encryption, XRP recent bull market ushered in
the short-term, permanent futures contracts traded on the BitMEX XRP is
increasing. However, as the news reports in the community, interest XRP could
fall.

a trader said that if BitMEX is an American Stock Exchange, will certainly be
subject to authorities to investigate the causes of the collapse of flash to
ensure no occurrence of such manipulation.

“does not roll back or refund”

Despite some traders claim that flash collapse when no trigger stop-loss, but
most other traders longs is still active, the liquidation of its assets did
not collapse during the flash. Complaints not stop trigger is reasonable
became a problem.

BitMEX posted on its website a statement, claiming that the collapse was
caused by the flash market factors, such as liquidity of the currency pair
lower, BitMEX team has investigated , and that there is no problem to stop the
exchange system, so they will not roll back or refund.
