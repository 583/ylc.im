Anti-block chain "plague" mountain fiscal largest R & D acquisition epidemic monitoring system

Recently, the novel coronavirus infection prevention and control of pneumonia
epidemic control work has entered a critical period. In the fight against the
epidemic, a big data, cloud computing, artificial intelligence, new generation
of information technology is playing a role, block chain technology with its
unique distributed, privacy, and other characteristics can not be tampered
with, the addition of anti “plague” army . Reporters learned, Shandong
University of Finance and Economics developed the “block chain acquisition
epidemic monitoring system” was 2020 at 5:00 on February 7 formally launched,
took just two days and nights.

2019 October expedite the implementation of the block chain technological
innovation and industrial development of a national strategy. Needed epidemic,
Shandong Province, the preparatory group block chain Finance Lab Science and
Technology Department of Shandong University of Finance, Ali clouds and other
units, research and development of the “block chain epidemic acquisition and
monitoring system.” The system proved block chain from three aspects can play
an irreplaceable role in the fight against the new coronavirus.

First, the system uses a fast and flexible, easy-to-deploy distributed
promotion. The biggest difference cloud computing, using the block chains peer
configuration, each unit may be added as a node to the block chains to share
data, encrypted storage, traceability and the responsibilities traceability.
Mountain financial big block chain system proved a node deployment after about
two days, you can start and join block chain. This distributed computing and
storage to maximize protect the system from damage caused by attacks and
failures; decentralized network structure can be resolved in a short time to
large concurrent data access, suitable for short epidemic acquisition time,
high occupant density characteristics.

Next, the trusted system through data encryption, to achieve privacy
protection, and sharing of traceability effect. Currently the national
infectious disease reporting integrity of the data, the accuracy of the high
requirements, the need to pay attention to data privacy in the collection, to
guard against abuse, thus creating data silos each acquisition or direct
reporting system. Data encryption block chain congenital transmission, and
synchronized to each node to establish a distributed network of data storage
between units, effectively solve the problem of data silos, improve data
collection society as a whole outbreaks of infectious diseases and real-time
early warning capacity for self do not rely entirely on national infectious
disease early warning system. Meanwhile, data traceability features can form a
complete, tamper-proof accountable mechanisms to ensure that data based on
transparency and supervision of accountable events.

Finally, the system can be keyed open the epidemic data, so that data is more
true epidemic. It is understood that the block chain technology as a core
technology of the next generation of the Internet, with a decentralized, open
and transparent, can not be tampered with, traceability and other features,
has a natural advantage in the fight against SARS epidemic prevention. School
of Computer Science and Technology, Shandong University of Finance and
Economics from 2010 peer network research, the current through the block chain
digital certificate, IPFS storage network, to gain advantage quantitative
analysis and design aspects of the transaction. Using the block chain key
technology (similar purse), permission for data storage, and better protect
the security of data; data on the block chain certification, can not be
altered, they are able to guarantee the authenticity of the epidemic data;
sensitive desensitization treatment after data storage, protection of privacy,
open sharing of data to achieve the epidemic.
